CPRLI Research

CPRLI is a multidisciplinary team of social scientists, engineers, and researchers who are committed to making a positive impact on the world through our research.

  • 01 CPRLI not only provides guidance to young talent but also involve in Empirical Research work, Policy analysis and Evaluation
  • 02 CPRLI conducts research on the impact of different policies on social and economic outcomes. This could include empirical research on the policy execution and implementation and its responses at ground level
  • 03 CPLRI organizes research seminars, conferences, workshops and other academic activities
  • 04CPLRI aims to collaborate with government ministries/organization/institution to carry out empirical research and consultancy on public policy and Implementation of law/s.

Project Proposals

  • 01 The Centre for Policy Research and Legal Initiative (CPRLI) involves in making inhouse projects which will be multidisciplinary in nature. We involve a team of cuttingedge social scientists, engineers, and researchers who are committed to bring positive impact on the world. Our Project will be focused on solving some of the most pressing challenges in social, economic, legal and public policy areas which our nation is facing today
  • 02CPRLI uses a variety of cutting-edge techniques and technologies to conduct research projects, and we aims to collaborate with leading experts from around the world
  • 03 CPRLI offer a variety of vocational and training programs and opportunities for students and early-career researchers
  • 04CPRLI also invites projects which can help to translate research into actionable recommendations for policymakers and practitioners

Conflict Management and Resolution

  • 01 CPRLI Conflict management services aims to settling of disputes outside of the courtroom.
  • 02 CPRLI believes in building team of experts to resolve disputes between parties by arriving at conclusion without a trial
  • 03 CPRLI provides solutions through arbitration, conciliation and mediation
  • 04CPRLI provides Legal Aid Services to the common people

Policy And Legal Consultancy

  • 01 CPRLI will promote public understanding of public policy and law
  • 02 CPRLI helps in developing policy briefs and white papers that explains the findings of research and makes recommendations for how policymakers can improve public policy
  • 03 CPRLI offers a variety of vocational and training programs and opportunities for students and early-career researchers
  • 04CPRLI organize dedicated seminar and conferences on Socio Political and Geo-political issues. CPRLI also provides one platform for discussion through conferences and seminars on events of national and international importance.

Data Center

  • 01 CPRLI collects data from various sources and help in developing educational materials, conducting public outreach events, and working with the media to communicate the findings of research to the public
  • 02 CPRLI provides a digital storehouse of Data which can be used for research and analysis, policymakers, and practitioners, and developing tools and resources that can be used to conduct public policy and law research
  • 03 CPRLI creates an online database of public policy research, various Policies, Acts, Guidelines, Regulations, Researches etc. which can useful and accessible for policy feedback

Facilitation Services

  • 01 CPRLI provides research and technical solutions not only to industries but also fulfill the requirements in social sector
  • 02CPRLI provides PPCP (Public Private Community Partnership) services for industry and social sector
  • 03 CPRLI provides a platform for everyone to come and discuss and find solutions through Seminars conducted at leading institutes in India
